Poetry: A Conversational Recommender System  

We want to recommend poems to learn through chat conversations.
The goal is to help users memorize and keep in memory as many classic poems as possible.
We must be able to accept both text and audio messages.

The general features are the following:
1. Ellicit user preferences by chatting.
2. Check user history and learner profile.
3. Choose appropriate items.
4. Check memorization.
5. Recommend an item.
6. Record the outcome of recommendation.
7. Keep track of memorization and help to revise.

The bot should be interesting enough to interact with. 
You can recommend classic poems in English and Russian.

Stack
FastAPI as backend.
Telegram bot as the frontend (Python).
Other choices are negotiable.

The project must:
- be deployable on a server (both bot and backend)
- be a public repo with MIT licence
- have a reproducible step-by-step deployment instructions in the readme.
